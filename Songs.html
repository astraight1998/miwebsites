LIFE SONGS

      These are my favorite songs because the message of the songs really touches my heart. Apart from the fact that Michael Jackson & Charice Pempengco are really good singers, I feel like the songs really speak to me on a spiritual level. I hope that you appreciate the message of the songs as I do and I also hope that you enjoy listening to it.
