HONDAGUA



      This is Hondagua, Lopez, Quezon. Hondagua is the name of the baranggay. Its name means "deep water" which is self-explanatory, it is definitely located near the beach. This is the province of my father. He spent his childhood there until he graduates elementary. Then they went to Manila, but they didn't sell the house in Quezon so it became a vacation house whenever we would spend our vacation there. We always go there every vacation in time to celebrate the fiesta which is celebrated on the 14th day of May but I haven't been able to go there since I entered college partly because of the academic calendar.



These are my favorite cousins on my father's side. Ate Ji (girl wearing blue), Ate Jen (girl wearing white), Kuya Kev (guy at the back) while Kuya Kyle (guy wearing blue), Kuya Benjie (guy carrying little girl) Brix (boy wearing yellow), IB (girl wearing yellow) & me (girl in the middle) lives in Manila & Bacoor. We've been close since we were kids until now. We always talk to each other from time to time and even though our cousins in Quezon already have their own families, they still make time to bond with us together with their kids. We would always go to the pool or beach whenever we go there but since our house is near the beach, we mostly go there.
